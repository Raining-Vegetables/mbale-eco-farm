What is Agriguard

  1. Agriguard is an affordable 2 in 1 organic fertilizer and pestice.
  2. Visible improvements in plant health within days, boosting photosynthesis.
  3. It increases crop resilience during heavy rains and hot weather, preventing damage and wilting.
  4. Enhances fruiting, prevents flower and fruit abortion, and promotes branching for better yields.
