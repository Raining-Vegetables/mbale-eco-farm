Our Story

At Mbale Ecological Farm, situated in Mbale, Uganda, we're driven by a profound commitment to sustainable agriculture and innovative farming practices since our inception in 2015. Through strategic collaborations with leading agricultural organizations, we've become recognized for our cutting-edge research and development in organic fertilizers and pesticides, tailored to enhance crop yield and promote environmental sustainability. With a vast 200-acre farm, we offer a diverse range of farm-to-table produce, ensuring freshness and nutritional value for our customers. Join us in our mission to redefine agriculture through our dedication to organic farming and ecological stewardship



About Us

Welcome to Mbale Ecological Farm, where innovation meets tradition in sustainable agriculture. Nestled in the heart of Mbale, Uganda, we are dedicated to revolutionizing farming practices through our commitment to organic methods and community empowerment.
