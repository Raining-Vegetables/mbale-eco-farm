Who are We?

Our Story

Mbale Ecological Farm in Uganda has promoted sustainable agriculture since 2015. Known for innovative organic fertilizers and pesticides, they collaborate with top agricultural organizations. Their 200-acre farm offers fresh, nutritious farm-to-table produce, redefining agriculture with a commitment to organic farming and environmental stewardship.
